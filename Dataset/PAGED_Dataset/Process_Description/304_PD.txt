For the supplier, the first step is to pick up the order. After picking up the order, the next step is to request a stock check. Once the stock check is requested, the inventory should be checked. If the stock is rejected, then the process ends. Otherwise, the supplier should request shipping details. After requesting the shipping details, they should receive the shipping details. At the same time, the supplier should also send the goods and send the invoice. Once the payment is received, the process ends.
