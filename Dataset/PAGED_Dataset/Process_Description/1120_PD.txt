The process begins with receiving a Ministerial enquiry, followed by assigning the enquiry to a minister. After assigning the enquiry, the next step is to investigate it. Once the investigation is complete, the ministerial response is prepared and then reviewed. If the response is not approved, the Ministerial correspondence is addressed and the process comes to an end. However, if the response is approved, the process continues.
