To begin the process, start with Start, followed by Pre-admission (T1). After Pre-admission (T1), proceed to Patient admission (T2). Once Patient admission (T2) is completed, move on to Detection and treatment (T3). Following Detection and treatment (T3), proceed to Discharge (T4). After Discharge (T4), it is time for Follow up (T5). Finally, after Follow up (T5), the process comes to an end.
