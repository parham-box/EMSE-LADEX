1. Start the procedure.
2. Wear a [SAFETY_DEVICE] to protect the equipment. Connect the device to the [GROUND_POINT] on the [CHASSIS].
3. Identify the [MODULE] raising the alarm.

How are you performing the procedure?  If using Command Line Interface go to step 5, if Graphical User Interface then go to step 30

5. Identify the [MODULE] by entering [COMMAND] to show alarm details.
6. Refer to the [DOCUMENT_A].

30. Retrieve active alarm data.
31. Refer to [DOCUMENT_B].

7. What type of [MODULE] is reported missing?

If External equipment is missing then go to step 8
If Internal module is missing then go to step 10

8. Verify that the cable between the [CHASSIS_EXTERNAL_PORT] and the external peripheral is connected and not damaged.
9. If the alarm does not clear, contact your next level of support or your [VENDOR] support group.

10. Is the slot empty or is the correct [MODULE] type present?

If Slot is empty then go to step 11
If Correct module type is present then go to step 15

11. Insert a [MODULE] of the correct type into the slot.
12. Wait 30 seconds and retrieve all alarms to determine if the original alarm has cleared.
13. Has the original alarm cleared?

If Alarm has cleared then go to step 14
If Alarm has not cleared then go to step 15

14. The procedure is complete.

15. Is the [MODULE] still carrying traffic?

If Module is carrying traffic then go to  step 16
If Module is not carrying traffic then go to step 29

16. Reseat the previously active [MODULE_TYPE_A].
17. Refer to the [DOCUMENT_C] procedure.
18. Initiate a switch back to the originally active [MODULE_TYPE_A].
19. Has the original alarm cleared?

If Alarm has cleared then go to  step 20
If Alarm has not cleared then go to step 21

20. The procedure is complete.
21. Ensure that the fan module is fully engaged with the backplane.
22. Refer to the [DOCUMENT_D] procedure.
23. If the alarm does not clear, replace the original [MODULE_TYPE_A].
24. Refer to the [DOCUMENT_E] procedure.
25. Initiate a switch back to the newly inserted [MODULE_TYPE_A].
26. Has the alarm cleared?

If Alarm has cleared then go to step 27
If Alarm has not cleared then go to step 28

27. The procedure is complete.
28. If the alarm does not clear, contact your next level of support or your [VENDOR] support group.
29. If the alarm does not clear, contact your next level of support or your [VENDOR] support group.
